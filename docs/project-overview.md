# Project Overview

## What is ARIELA?

ARIELA is a mobile application for iOS and Android dedicated to women's health. It accompanies girls and women through every life stage: first period, cycle tracking, menstrual hygiene, pregnancy prevention, fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum recovery.

The goal is to be smarter than a simple calendar app: educational, personalized, discreet, modern, and AI-assisted.

## The problem we solve

Many young girls and women do not fully understand their cycle, their symptoms, hygiene during periods, fertility windows, or pregnancy-related changes.

ARIELA aims to provide a safe space to:

- Understand one's body
- Track menstrual cycles accurately
- Receive reliable health information
- Plan or prevent pregnancy
- Follow a pregnancy week by week
- Be supported through postpartum recovery

## Why ARIELA is different

Most existing women's health apps fall into one of two categories:

1. **Basic period trackers** — Simple calendars without education or personalization
2. **Premium-priced US apps** — Expensive, English-only, designed for Western markets

ARIELA is positioned to fill the gap:

- **Bilingual (French + English) from day one** — serving Europe and French-speaking Africa equally
- **Educational at every stage** — not just tracking, but teaching
- **AI-assisted** — personalized guidance, available 24/7
- **Affordable** — freemium model with a generous free tier
- **Privacy-first** — no ads, no data selling, EU-hosted backend

## Target markets

Primary launch markets:

- France, Belgium, Switzerland (FR-speaking Europe)
- Democratic Republic of Congo, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire (FR-speaking Africa)
- Canada, UK, US (EN-speaking)

Secondary markets (post-launch):

- Turkey (where the developer is based — local context)
- Other EU countries (German, Spanish, Italian translations later)

## Project goals

### Product goals

1. Ship a polished, App Store-ready MVP within 4-6 months
2. Cover 6 distinct life-stage modules, each genuinely useful
3. Achieve a 4.5+ star rating on launch
4. Acquire 1,000+ users in the first 3 months
5. Achieve 5%+ free-to-premium conversion rate

### Portfolio goals

1. Demonstrate full-stack mobile development capabilities
2. Showcase production-grade architecture and documentation
3. Showcase product thinking, UX/UI, AI integration
4. Serve as a flagship project for career and freelance opportunities

## Success criteria for v1

ARIELA v1 is considered successful when:

- ✅ Available on both App Store and Google Play
- ✅ Fully bilingual (FR + EN) with no translation gaps
- ✅ Free tier is genuinely useful without paywall pressure
- ✅ Premium tier converts at 5%+ from trial users
- ✅ All 6 modules are functional and tested
- ✅ AI assistant provides safe, helpful, properly-disclaimed guidance
- ✅ Privacy and security meet GDPR + Apple HealthKit-equivalent standards
- ✅ Average load time under 2 seconds on a 3G connection
